EXPLORE OUR CAREER PATHWAYS

Whatever your goals—a degree or certificate, job training or transfer to a university—Yakima Valley College offers quality educational programs at an affordable cost to help you reach them.

YVC offers over 55 professional/technical degrees, 100+ certificates and five bachelor of applied science degrees to prepare you for success in your career and life. Through our six academic pathways, YVC provides a “road map” to help you chart your path from your college journey into your next chapter.
